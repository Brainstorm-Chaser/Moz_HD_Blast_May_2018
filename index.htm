MOZ - Elements Pallette

Metal Surfacing Solutions Inspired by the Elements

This spring, Oakland-based metal experts, Móz Designs, are using the forces of nature as the inspiration for their newest color palette. Variegated tones of steel, brass, bronze, and copper form the foundation of the Elements palette, which can be used to add timeworn textures to walls, columns, ceilings and facades.


The look of naturally weathered steel and aged brass, bronze or copper is achieved through advanced printing technology. High-res colors are captured digitally then printed in-house on solid-core aluminum sheet metal. This process allows designers to achieve the look of natural metals without the price, weight, or raw surface residue.


Elements colorways can be enriched in-house with CNC engravings, handcrafted grains, and laser cutting techniques for a unique material solution ideal for hospitality environments. Móz’s refined metal collections are engineered with the value, durability, and longevity to withstand the test of time.
